About Audacity_Voice

Audacity_Voice is a free, customised version of Audacity 1.2.6. It enhances the voice-recording capabilities for users of Audiotouch.

Audiotouch is a voice recording program which reduces user keystroke inputs to the minimum. Audacity_Voice also minimises keystrokes for the saving of edited files.

Audacity_Voice is made possible by the entire Audacity Team. Specific mods for this version of Audacity were made by Vaughan Johnson. Students of the World, with a microphone in hand, whose computing experiences are enlivened by voice recordings, will no doubt join me in expressing gratitude to these guys!
